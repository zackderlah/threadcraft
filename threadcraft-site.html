Things people
actually ask.
How good is the output, actually?
Most users in the beta edit 1–2 tweets before posting, not the whole thread. The hooks are consistently the strongest part — it's been trained specifically on what performs. You'll want to add your own voice and specific examples, but it gets you 80% of the way there instantly.
Does it post to X for me?
Not yet. ThreadCraft outputs your thread to the clipboard, ready to paste into X, Typefully, or Buffer. Direct posting and scheduler integration are on the roadmap for the next major update.
What counts as one thread?
Each time you click Generate, that counts as one thread. Regenerating with a different tone is another thread. Editing tweets after generation doesn't count — edit as much as you want.
How is this different from Typefully or Taplio?
Typefully and Taplio are full content platforms — scheduling, analytics, CRM, at $29–49/month. ThreadCraft is a focused tool that does one thing better. It's a $9 knife, not a Swiss Army toolkit. If you already use those platforms, ThreadCraft's output pastes directly into them.
What's the refund policy?
If you're not happy in the first 7 days, email us and we'll refund you immediately. No forms, no questions.
